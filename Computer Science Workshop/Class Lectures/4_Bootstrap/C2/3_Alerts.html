Alerts

Success! Such an alert generates the result of a positive or successful activity.
Info! Such an alert describes a piece of information which can neither be categorized as positive or negative.
Warning! Such an alert generates a warning for the user.
Danger! Such an alert describes the presence of a negative action.
Primary! Such an alert describes important information.
Secondary! Such an alert describes low priority information.
Dark! Such an alert has a dark grey background.
Light! Such an alert a light green background.
